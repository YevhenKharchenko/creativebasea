About The Game

Carrom Party is basically the classic table game, but digital - it's strategic when you want it to be, but you can also just jump in and play. Honestly, it took us forever to figure out how to make this work on such a small screen - turns out, simple controls and quick games are the way to go. Whether you've got 30 seconds or 30 minutes, this game works.

How To Play

The game is played by tapping and dragging to aim your striker, then releasing to flick and pocket pieces. Success depends on precision and positioning rather than raw power. Matches are fast-paced, but thoughtful aiming is key to winning.

  • Tap and drag to aim

  • Release to flick and pocket pieces

  • Precision matters more than force

  • Simple controls designed for Wear OS
